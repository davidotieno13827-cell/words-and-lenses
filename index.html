The Man Behind The Lens

Storyteller. Filmmaker. Author. Humanitarian.

My name is Hillary Ominde, a passionate storyteller, filmmaker, author, youth empowerment advocate, and humanitarian dedicated to creating meaningful impact through media, education, and community service.

I am the author of Soul & Solace, a book that explores personal growth, resilience, healing, and the transformative power of hope. Through my writing, I seek to inspire individuals to embrace their journeys and discover purpose even in challenging circumstances.

My professional journey spans filmmaking, videography, content creation, production management, event organization, digital marketing, customer relations, and talent management. Over the years, I have worked with organizations, ministries, media productions, podcasts, and community initiatives across different sectors, helping bring stories to life through visual storytelling and strategic communication.

As a filmmaker and freelance videographer, I specialize in documenting authentic human experiences, social impact projects, community initiatives, and inspiring personal stories. My work combines creativity with purpose, ensuring that every story not only informs but also inspires action.

Beyond media, I am deeply committed to community development and youth empowerment. I have actively supported educational initiatives for underprivileged children in Bomet County, advocating for access to quality education and opportunities for vulnerable communities. My passion for social impact has also seen me participate in various charitable activities aimed at uplifting lives and empowering future generations.

I serve as a Child Rights Ambassador and remain actively involved in advocacy efforts that promote the well-being, protection, and empowerment of children and young people.

My diverse experience also includes serving as a Production Manager, Podcast Studio Volunteer, Tour Guide, Travel Blogger, Chauffeur, and Event Organizer. These roles have strengthened my ability to connect with people from different backgrounds, manage complex projects, and build meaningful relationships across communities.

Through my NGO initiative, Transform Kenya, I continue to champion sustainable community development, youth empowerment, storytelling, and social transformation. My vision is to use media, education, and advocacy as tools to inspire positive change and create opportunities for those who need them most.
