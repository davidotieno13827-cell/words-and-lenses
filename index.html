The Man Behind The Lens

Storyteller. Filmmaker. Author. Humanitarian.

My name is Hillary Ominde, a passionate storyteller, filmmaker, author, youth empowerment advocate, and humanitarian dedicated to creating meaningful impact through media, education, and community service.

I am the author of Soul & Solace, a book that explores personal growth, resilience, healing, and the transformative power of hope.

My professional journey spans filmmaking, videography, content creation, production management, event organization, digital marketing, customer relations, and talent management.

As a filmmaker and freelance videographer, I specialize in documenting authentic human experiences, social impact projects, community initiatives, and inspiring personal stories.

Beyond media, I am deeply committed to community development and youth empowerment. I have actively supported educational initiatives for underprivileged children in Bomet County.

I serve as a Child Rights Ambassador and remain actively involved in advocacy efforts that promote the well-being, protection, and empowerment of children and young people.

Through my NGO initiative, Transform Kenya, I continue to champion sustainable community development, youth empowerment, storytelling, and social transformation.
